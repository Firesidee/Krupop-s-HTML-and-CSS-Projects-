Parrots

Learn all about Parrots, both wild and tame and their habitats!

Amazon Parrot

(Blue Fronted Amazon pictured)
Amazon Parrots mostly come in shades of green and are moderate sized birds that should only be left in the care of intermediate to advanced bird owners.

Wikipedia Pet Amazon Parrot Care

Macaw

(Blue and Gold Macaws pictured)
Macaws are beautiful large birds, but should either be left alone in the wild or left to advanced bird keepers

Wikipedia Pet Macaw Care

Lorikeet

(Rainbow Lorikeet Pictured)
Lorikeets are very special parrots as one part of their diet is pollen. They have a bristled tongue to aid them in harvesting the pollen from flowers. Lorikeets are a small parrot.

Wikipedia Pet Lorikeet care
